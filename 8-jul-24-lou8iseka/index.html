BMI Description

BMI (Body Mass Index) is a measurement used to determine whether your weight is within a healthy range. This calculator should only be used by adults (pregnant or breastfeeding women should not rely on this BMI reading) and no action should be taken based on it. its value in addition to consulting a qualified person such as a doctor. This calculator will give you an idea of ​​how your weight compares to general values. Body Mass Index is calculated as your weight (in kilograms) divided by the square of your height (in meters).
